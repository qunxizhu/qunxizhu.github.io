About Me ([ResearchGate] [Google Scholar] [Twitter] [Resume])

    Hi! I am Qunxi Zhu, a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Research Institute of Intelligent Complex Systems, Fudan University, China. I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Fudan University, advised by Professor Wei Lin (杰青、教务处处长).

    Recently, my research interests lie in complex systems and machine learning:
  • Machine learning for complex systems: promoting the development of complex systems with machine learning;
  • Complex systems for machine learning: developing advanced machine learning techniques inspired by the theories and methods of complex systems.
    • My work, as the first author or corresponding author, has been published in top international journals such as Nature Communications, Research, Physical Review Research, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (IEEE-TAC), SIAM Journal on Control and Optimization (SIAM-CON), Systems & Control Letters (SCL), CHAOS as well as top artificial intelligence conferences like ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, and AAAI.
